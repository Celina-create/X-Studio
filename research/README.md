# Research

> **What this folder is** — Field reports and ecosystem snapshots that growth operators can read before placing a bet.

Most "research" online is either (a) journalist-style overviews that are too shallow to act on, or (b) gated industry reports that cost USD 2,000 and arrive 6 months late. **X-Studio research is the third option** — synthesis written by an operator, for operators, while the signal is still actionable.

Every report here follows the same structure:

| Section | What it answers |
|---|---|
| **What's happening** | Raw events / launches / conversations, sourced and timestamped |
| **What it means** | Operator interpretation — strategic signal, not just news |
| **Capability map** | What the ecosystem can / cannot do today, by maturity rating |
| **Takeaways for builders** | 3–5 actionable bets you could make on this signal |

## Methodology

How research gets into this folder.

1. **Live signal first.** Reports start from real public events (livestreams, launches, AMA threads) — not from re-reading other people's blog posts.
2. **Primary capture, then synthesis.** Each event is captured raw (transcript, screenshot, demo notes) before any interpretation gets layered on top.
3. **Capability matrix as the spine.** Every report ends with a maturity rating per capability, so a reader can answer "is X a real moat or a demo trick?" in 30 seconds.
4. **Takeaways must be actionable.** "Interesting" doesn't make it into the takeaways. "You could ship Y and capture Z" does.

---

## Source disclosure

All reports cite original sources — public livestreams, open-source GitHub repos, public Twitter posts, public WeChat articles. **No leaked or NDA-protected material ever lands here.** Where reports anonymize internal operators or company-specific examples, it's to keep the lens general — not to obscure the source.
